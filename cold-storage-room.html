Ripening Chambers

ripening chambers

Ripening Chambers are designed for controlled ripening of fruits like bananas and mangoes. These chambers are fitted with automatic temperature and humidity control for uniform ripening, preserving the taste and quality of fruits.

Special Features

  1. Automatic temperature and humidity control: Ensures uniform ripening and quality maintenance
  2. Robust construction: Suitable for heavy-duty use
  3. Energy-efficient refrigeration systems: Reduces operational costs
  4. Advanced control systems: for monitoring and adjusting parameters
  5. Safety features: include alarms for temperature deviations

Essential Requirements

  • Air-tight room with insulation
  • Temperature control system for cooling/heating
  • Air circulation and ventilation system
  • Humidity control
  • Ethylene gas injection system
  • Electric control system
